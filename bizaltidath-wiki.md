“Bizaltidath originates from the [volcano](https://dragon-adventures.fandom.com/wiki/Volcano) world and formed by the volcanoes themselves. Through many decades, these dragons have been existing to only protect their homeland from any disturbance. The rock-like scales throughout their body are thick and creates a great defense to their real body. They love to bathe in lava pools along with [magmips](https://dragon-adventures.fandom.com/wiki/Magmip), since their scales can also protect them from the heat. Bizaltidath’s appearance may look tough but they have a soft spot for anything that shows kindness to them. Therefore, the adventurer will have a tough companion but a great friend.”
The Bizaltidath can be hatched from the following [egg(s)](https://dragon-adventures.fandom.com/wiki/Eggs):

The Bizaltidath has a **10%** chance of hatching from the [Volcano Egg](https://dragon-adventures.fandom.com/wiki/Volcano_Egg).
The [Lucky Egg](https://www.roblox.com/game-pass/8510023/Lucky-Egg) gamepass does not affect the Bizaltidath's hatch chance.
The other dragons exclusive to the Volcano Egg are the:

- [Venu](https://dragon-adventures.fandom.com/wiki/Venu), with a **25%** chance of hatching.
- [Numine](https://dragon-adventures.fandom.com/wiki/Numine), with a **50%** chance of hatching.
- [Magmip](https://dragon-adventures.fandom.com/wiki/Magmip), with a **15%** chance of hatching.The Bizaltidath has a **20%** chance of hatching from the [Imbued Fireworks Egg 2025](https://dragon-adventures.fandom.com/wiki/Imbued_Fireworks_Egg_2025).
The [Lucky Egg](https://www.roblox.com/game-pass/8510023/Lucky-Egg) gamepass does not affect the Bizaltidath's hatch chance.
The other dragons exclusive to the Imbued Fireworks Egg 2025 are the:

- [Numine](https://dragon-adventures.fandom.com/wiki/Numine), with a **30%** chance of hatching.
- [Venu](https://dragon-adventures.fandom.com/wiki/Venu), with a **30%** chance of hatching.
- [Magmip](https://dragon-adventures.fandom.com/wiki/Magmip), with a **20%** chance of hatching.
Additionally, the Bizaltidath has a **10%** chance to hatch with the Celestial Blaze [cosmetic trait](https://dragon-adventures.fandom.com/wiki/Cosmetic_traits).The Bizaltidath has a **20%** chance of hatching from the [Imbued Fireworks Egg 2026](https://dragon-adventures.fandom.com/wiki/Imbued_Fireworks_Egg_2026).
With the [Lucky Egg](https://www.roblox.com/game-pass/8510023/Lucky-Egg) gamepass, the Bizaltidath has a **25%** chance of hatching.
The other dragons exclusive to the Imbued Fireworks Egg 2026 are the:

- [Numine](https://dragon-adventures.fandom.com/wiki/Numine), with a **30%** chance of hatching.
- [Dexyn](https://dragon-adventures.fandom.com/wiki/Dexyn), with a **30%** chance of hatching.

Additionally, the Bizaltidath has a **10%** chance to hatch with the Celestial Blaze [cosmetic trait](https://dragon-adventures.fandom.com/wiki/Cosmetic_traits) and a **5%** chance to hatch with the Auspicious Clouds [element](https://dragon-adventures.fandom.com/wiki/Elements).The Bizaltidath **is able** to be [bred](https://dragon-adventures.fandom.com/wiki/Breeding) only if the player has unlocked [Volcano](https://dragon-adventures.fandom.com/wiki/Volcano). At level 1, it has a 1-hour, 29-minute and 17-second breeding cooldown, and at level 50, it has a 54-minute breeding cooldown. It has a **350** coin [breeding cost](https://dragon-adventures.fandom.com/wiki/Breeding#Breeding_Cost).

The same rules apply to [Co-Hatching](https://dragon-adventures.fandom.com/wiki/Co-Hatching).The minimum starting [bid](https://dragon-adventures.fandom.com/wiki/Auction_House#Auctioning) is **10,000** coins.
The auto buy has the same limit.

The Bizaltidath **is able** to be [traded](https://dragon-adventures.fandom.com/wiki/Trading).
