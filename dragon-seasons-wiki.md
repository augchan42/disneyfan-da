# Dragon Seasons

**Dragon Seasons**, simply known as seasons, last 28 days (4 weeks) and contain 50 free tier rewards and 50 bonus premium tier rewards, for a total of 100 tiers which can be unlocked by reaching Season Point milestones. Season Points can be earned by completing Daily or Weekly missions. The player can skip a tier for 29 Robux. Users can access the Season Pass via the right side of their screen or via the 4 hotkey on PC and within the Menu on mobile.

A Season begins on the first Friday of each month at 5:00 PM EDT (4:00 PM EST when daylight savings time is not in effect), lasts 28 days, and ends on a Friday at the aforementioned time.

## Passes

There are three kinds of passes available in the Season Pass.

### Free Pass

The Free Pass, as its name suggests, contains 50 tiers available for all players to complete. Tier 50 is the final tier of the Free Pass and contains either new season dragon for the player to choose between. The player may only pick one of the season dragons.

### Dragoneer Pass

The Dragoneer Pass is a premium pass purchasable for 499 Robux and contains some exclusive rewards not found in the Free Pass or elsewhere and also contains the Free Pass' rewards.

The Dragoneer Pass also allows the player to claim the legacy season dragon at tier 50 from the same month from last year.

### Drakon Pass

The Drakon Pass is a premium pass purchasable for 749 Robux and contains some exclusive rewards not found in the Free Pass, Dragoneer Pass, or elsewhere and also contains the Free Pass and Dragoneer Pass' rewards.

The Drakon Pass also allows the player to complete tiers 51-100 and two additional chances at claiming either new season dragon at tier 50 and tier 100.
