# Potions

*See also: Presets*

Potions are items with various ways of obtainment and are used to alter dragons in a variety of ways. Potions may be salvaged, sold, and traded as well. Potions may be viewed in the player's inventory underneath the Potions tab.

Each potion has a maximum capacity of 100 (150 if the Big Backpack gamepass is owned). With no upgrades, 10 potions may also be stored at the Bank.

## Obtainment & Usage

Potions can be obtained in various ways. Most regular potions are obtainable from chests, through Alchemy, and Spinner Wheels. Potions are also purchasable from the General Store and the Player Market in the Undercity. Some potions are able to be fished, won from Tundra races, or won from defeating bosses.

Potions are also obtainable from the Daily Login and Daily Task rewards, in some Weekly Missions, and in the free and Dragon Pass rewards during Dragon Seasons. Event potions are usually purchasable from event stores or can be obtained through other means.

The player can use a potion on a dragon by accessing its "Feed" menu in the Lair or through its interaction menu and then navigating to the "Potions" tab. The player can then select any available and applicable potion to use on the dragon.
