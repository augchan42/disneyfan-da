## Phase 1: Veidreki Token (URGENT — event ends April 3, 2026)

> **This is the time-limited piece. Cannot be obtained after the event ends.**

### How to get it

Collect **36 Stormbound Eggs** → earns 1 Veidreki Token via the Event Pass meter.

### Daily egg sources

| Source | Eggs | Time | Notes |
|--------|------|------|-------|
| 3 Daily missions | 3 | Quick | 1 egg each, refresh daily at 4:00 PM EST |
| 7 Repeatable missions | 14 | ~35 min | 2 eggs each, 5 min cooldown |
| Event Pass tiers 3, 6, 9, 10 | 4 | Free | Unlocked by completing 10 missions above |
| **Daily total** | **21** | **~40 min** | |

### Timeline

- At 21 eggs/day → **~2 days** to hit 36 eggs
- She has **15 days** until the event ends — tons of buffer
- Even at a lazy pace (just dailies + a few repeatables), she can get this done in a week

### Minimum daily routine

1. Log in, do 3 daily missions (~10 min)
2. Do 7 repeatable missions (~35 min, 5 min cooldown each)
3. Collect Event Pass rewards (tiers 3, 6, 9, 10 give Stormbound Eggs)
4. Done for the day

### If homework is heavy

Even just doing the 3 dailies + 4 repeatables (~7 missions, ~20 min) gets her:
- 3 + 8 = 11 eggs from missions
- Hits tier 7 of Event Pass → 2 bonus eggs (tiers 3 and 6)
- **13 eggs/day** → 36 eggs in ~3 days

She does NOT need to no-life this. A few days of casual play is enough.
