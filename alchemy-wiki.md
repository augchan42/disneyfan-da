# Alchemy

Alchemy is a feature that allows players to create potions through recycling food, resources, or other potions. Its associated building, "Alchemy Junkers," can be found to the left of the Spinner Wheel in Origins.

In the Factory, items can be salvaged to create Alchemy Elixir. The number of resources required for a single Elixir is relative to the item's rarity. Alchemy Elixir can also be obtained through Dragon Season and Daily Login rewards, as well as silver and gold chests.

At the Brewery, there are always ten different potions available for players to brew. Each potion requires a specific amount of Alchemy Elixir, with rarer potions requiring larger amounts. Additionally, all potions require an Empty Bottle, which can be obtained through Weekly Missions, Dragon Season and Daily Login rewards, and silver and gold chests. Alternatively, four empty bottles may be purchased for 350 Robux at the Brewery.

The potions in the Brewery rotate every twenty-four hours along with local chests, Daily Missions, and the Spinner Wheel.

## Titles

Three titles can be obtained by players from brewing potions with Alchemy:

- 100 Potions Brewed - Apprentice Alchemist
- 500 Potions Brewed - Adept Alchemist
- 1,000 Potions Brewed - Supreme Alchemist

## Trivia

- Alchemy was introduced alongside the third rendition of the Origins world.
- During a Guild War, one of the tasks for players is to alchemy 40 Elixir in the Factory. This task can be completed up to three times, granting 10 Guild Points each. The following list shows the number of resources needed by rarity:
  - 10,000 common resources.
  - 2,000 uncommon resources.
  - 1,000 rare resources.
  - 400 epic resources.
  - 120 legendary resources.
