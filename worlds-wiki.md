# Worlds

Worlds are realms accessible via the World Teleporter in Dragon Adventures. Worlds are divided into "adventure" and "non-adventure", with the former having unique items and the latter having no "unique" items. Each adventure world has different resources, foods, dragons, and eggs that can be obtained, as well as world-specific mobs and bosses.

New players will always start the game in the Origins world. To unlock new worlds, the player must complete all six World Missions in their latest unlocked world at least once to be able to unlock the next world. Undercity will always be unlocked at the start of the game.

Most events take place in exclusive worlds that are only accessible during their runtime.

## List of Worlds

There are currently twelve permanent worlds in Dragon Adventures.

1. Origins
2. Undercity
3. Grassland
4. Jungle
5. Volcano
6. Tundra
7. Ocean
8. Desert
9. Fantasy
10. Wasteland
11. Prehistoric
12. Shinrin

## Trivia

- The Undercity has many differences compared to the other adventure worlds.
  - There are no mobs nor bosses present.
  - The world lacks unique eggs, dragons, resources, and foods (excluding the fish, which it reuses from Origins).
  - It is the only adventure world that has an obtainable element potion.
  - It is the only adventure world with two entrances: one through Origins, and the other through teleportation. (This is no longer true as of the Origins revamp on 1 August 2025; you can only teleport out of Undercity using the teleporter that takes you to Origins, but you cannot do the same from the Origins side as it's not used to teleport you to bases/plots.)
- Prior to September 28th, 2024, the player must have hatched all of the dragons from the previous world before they could unlock the next. World dragons obtained from trading did not count towards new world unlocks.
