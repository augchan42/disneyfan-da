"The Ferorex are a rare, desert dwelling wyvern known for their incredible ferocity. What truly sets the Ferorex apart from other dragons is their astonishing bite strength, which surpasses that of any other dragon. Despite the below average size of this species, they fearlessly engage in battles with dragons much larger than themselves. Their preferred method of dispatching prey is by crushing their victim's skulls with their powerful jaws. This wyvern also has a distinctive fatty tail, which is used to store essential nutrients, ensuring their survival during times of scarcity. Despite their violent nature in the wild, the Ferorex when tamed is described as laid back, and friendly, as long as they are well fed. Though their wild side will quickly return if their trainer is in danger."
The Ferorex can be hatched from the following [egg(s)](https://dragon-adventures.fandom.com/wiki/Eggs):

The Ferorex has a **6%** chance of hatching from the [Desert Egg](https://dragon-adventures.fandom.com/wiki/Desert_Egg).
With the [Lucky Egg](https://www.roblox.com/game-pass/8510023/Lucky-Egg) gamepass, the Ferorex has a **12%** chance of hatching.
The other dragons exclusive to the Desert Egg are the:

- [Dunvolth](https://dragon-adventures.fandom.com/wiki/Dunvolth), with a **2%** chance of hatching.
- [Khepera](https://dragon-adventures.fandom.com/wiki/Khepera), with a **46%** chance of hatching.
- [Agricos](https://dragon-adventures.fandom.com/wiki/Agricos), with a **46%** chance of hatching.The Ferorex has a **25%** chance of hatching from the [Sun Egg 2025](https://dragon-adventures.fandom.com/wiki/Sun_Egg_2025).
With the [Lucky Egg](https://www.roblox.com/game-pass/8510023/Lucky-Egg) gamepass, the Ferorex has a **22%** chance of hatching.
The other dragons exclusive to the Sun Egg 2025 are the:

- [Venu](https://dragon-adventures.fandom.com/wiki/Venu), with a **30%** chance of hatching.
- [Hexalios](https://dragon-adventures.fandom.com/wiki/Hexalios), with a **20%** chance of hatching.
- [Solarizon](https://dragon-adventures.fandom.com/wiki/Solarizon), with a **13%** chance of hatching.
- [Volkumos](https://dragon-adventures.fandom.com/wiki/Volkumos), with a **11%** chance of hatching.
- [Lunaesol](https://dragon-adventures.fandom.com/wiki/Lunaesol), with a **1%** chance of hatching.
Additionally, the Ferorex has a **5%** chance to hatch with the Black Hole or Solar Rays [cosmetic traits](https://dragon-adventures.fandom.com/wiki/Cosmetic_traits) and/or Gold [element](https://dragon-adventures.fandom.com/wiki/Elements), and a **2%** chance to hatch with the Messier element.The Ferorex **is able** to be [bred](https://dragon-adventures.fandom.com/wiki/Breeding) only if the player has unlocked [Desert](https://dragon-adventures.fandom.com/wiki/Desert). At level 1, it has a 2-hour, 18-minute, and 53-second breeding cooldown, and at level 50, it has a 1-hour and 24-minute breeding cooldown. It has a **750** coin [breeding cost](https://dragon-adventures.fandom.com/wiki/Breeding#Breeding_Cost).

The same rules apply to [Co-Hatching](https://dragon-adventures.fandom.com/wiki/Co-Hatching).The minimum starting [bid](https://dragon-adventures.fandom.com/wiki/Auction_House#Auctioning) is **10,000** coins.
The auto buy has the same limit.

The Ferorex **is able** to be [traded](https://dragon-adventures.fandom.com/wiki/Trading).
