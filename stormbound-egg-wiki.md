# Stormbound Egg

*Source: https://dragon-adventures.fandom.com/wiki/Stormbound_Egg*

---

The **Stormbound Egg** is an [egg](https://dragon-adventures.fandom.com/wiki/Eggs) obtainable from the [Veidreki Live Event](https://dragon-adventures.fandom.com/wiki/Veidreki_Live_Event).
Players are able to receive the Stormbound Egg through daily and repeatable quests, as well as from the Event Pass. One Stormbound Egg was also awarded for attending the one-time kick-off live event on March 13th, 2026, at 4:00PM EST.

## Trivia
On February 28th, a giant Stormbound Egg was added along with the island it rested on, located on the edge of [Origins](https://dragon-adventures.fandom.com/wiki/Origins). An Egg Watch feature was also added. Clicking it would show the  island with a static effect overlaying it. There was a button to return to the game, as well as a smaller text below that read "The egg may not be visible on lower graphics settings!"
The egg cracked a total of two times, rewarding users watching with different items each time.
- The egg first cracked on March 4th, 3:37 PM EST, rewarding the users watching with a Storm Theme Potion.
- The egg cracked again on March 9th, 6:12 PM EST, rewarding the users watching with a Lightning Theme Potion.
- On March 13th, at 4:20 PM EST, the egg hatched, with adult [Veidrekis](https://dragon-adventures.fandom.com/wiki/Veidreki) flying out of the egg and into [Origins](https://dragon-adventures.fandom.com/wiki/Origins). This also kick-started the [Veidreki Live Event](https://dragon-adventures.fandom.com/wiki/Veidreki_Live_Event).
