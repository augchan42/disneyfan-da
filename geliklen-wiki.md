"The Geliklen is a cautious and intelligent gecko-like dragon that thrives in the lush foliage of the Jungle World. Geliklens are omnivores, with a particular fondness for the nutritious and sugary fruits found within their habitat. In terms of combat, Geliklens are not particularly suited for intense battles due to their weak offensive and defensive strength. They are more effective when facing weaker opponents. The Geliklen's skittish nature prompts it to constantly seek shelter. It possesses wide, thin wings that allow it to glide gracefully above the canopy to easily find cover. Earning a Geliklen's trust is challenging due to them being naturally timid and cautious. However, those fortunate enough to gain the confidence of a Geliklen will be rewarded with a loyal and friendly lifelong companion."
The Geliklen can be hatched from the following [egg(s)](https://dragon-adventures.fandom.com/wiki/Eggs):
