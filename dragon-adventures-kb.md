# Dragon Adventures Knowledge Base

## Lair (Dragon Storage) Slots

### Overview
- The **Lair** (aka "My Dragons") holds all your dragons [^1]
- You start with **5 slots** (free) [^1]
- Each upgrade adds **+5 slots** [^1]
- Maximum capacity: **200 slots** (40 upgrades) [^1]
- **Loyalty Perks** (join Sonar Studios group + favorite the game): **+5 bonus slots** (205 total) [^1]
- Dragons in Stables don't count toward lair slots [^1]
- On February 2nd, 2024, lair upgrade costs were reduced and capacity was doubled [^1]

### Cost Scaling Pattern [^1]
- Costs increase roughly exponentially
- Early upgrades (1-3): very cheap (0-1k)
- Mid upgrades (4-12): moderate (5k-224k)
- Late upgrades (13-20): expensive (291k-1.16M)
- End-game upgrades (21-40): very expensive (1.35M-10M per upgrade)

## Gamepasses (Robux Purchases) [^3]

| Gamepass | Effect |
|----------|--------|
| **VIP** | Equip up to 3 dragons at once, faster walk/run, DOUBLE XP drop rates, gold chat text [^1][^3] |
| **Big Backpack** | 50% more item capacity (food, healing, resources, potions) [^2][^3] |
| **Multi-Accessory** | 3 accessories per body part instead of 2 [^4][^3] |

- Private server owners can equip up to 5 dragons in their server [^1]
- Big Backpack affects item stack sizes, NOT dragon lair slots [^2]

---

## Inventory (Items, not Dragons) [^2]

- Separate from the Lair (which is dragon storage) [^2]
- Each food/healing/resource item: capacity of 10,000 (15,000 with Big Backpack) [^2]
- Each egg: capacity of 10 [^2]
- Each potion: capacity of 100 (150 with Big Backpack) [^2]

## Analysis: Is 224k Gold Worth 5 Extra Slots? (60 → 65)

### The Facts
- **Current situation**: 60 slots
- **In-game cost for next 5 slots (60→65)**: 224,000 coins [^9]
- **Note**: The wiki lists this upgrade at 291k [^1], but the actual in-game price is 224k as confirmed by a player in March 2026. Prices may have been adjusted since the wiki was last updated.

### Cost Trajectory After 60 Slots
| Next Upgrade | New Capacity | Wiki Price | In-Game Price (if known) |
|-------------|-------------|-----------|--------------------------|
| 60→65 | 65 | 291,000 | **224,000** [^9] |
| 65→70 | 70 | 371,000 | TBD |
| 70→75 | 75 | 463,000 | TBD |

### Verdict
At 224k for 5 slots (44,800/slot), this is a **reasonable buy**. Whether it's worth it depends on:

1. **Are you actively running out of space?** If you're constantly at 58-60/60, yes — the convenience is worth it
2. **How fast do you earn coins?** If 291k takes you days of grinding, consider selling/trading duplicates instead
3. **Are you breeding?** Breeders need more slots. If you're collecting/hoarding, manage inventory instead
4. **Stables**: Check if Stables can offload some dragons you don't actively use
5. **Loyalty Perks**: Free +5 slots if you haven't claimed them (join group + favorite game)

### Recommendation
- **Claim Loyalty Perks first** (free 5 slots → effectively 65 without spending)
- If you still need more after that, 291k for 65→70 is reasonable in early-to-mid game
- Past 75 slots, costs become very steep (463k+) — only expand if you're actively breeding or collecting event dragons

## Tips for Coin Management
- Favoriting dragons protects them from accidental deletion/trade [^1]
- Delete/release unwanted dragons to free slots instead of buying more [^1]
- Use Stables to store dragons you don't need immediate access to [^1]
- Trade duplicate/low-value dragons for coins or better dragons [^8]
